Be the Change for Life Below Water - Volunteer With Us!

Our oceans are in danger! Pollution, overfishing, and climate change threaten marine life and coastal communities. But you can make a difference! Join us in protecting and restoring the blue heart of our planet.

Explore Volunteering Opportunities

Coastal Cleanup Drives

Country

Join a global movement to keep our beaches clean! Collect plastic waste, discarded fishing gear, and other pollutants that threaten marine life. Your efforts will help restore the beauty of our coastlines and protect sea creatures from harmful debris.

Coral Reef Restoration

Dive into action by helping restore fragile coral ecosystems! Assist marine biologists in planting coral fragments, monitoring reef health, and combating the effects of climate change. Together, we can rebuild vibrant underwater worlds teeming with life.

Ocean Awareness Campaigns

Be a voice for our oceans! Engage communities, schools, and online audiences through impactful storytelling, creative workshops, and advocacy campaigns. By educating others about marine conservation, you can inspire a new wave of ocean protectors.

Sustainable Fishing Education

Help fishermen adopt eco-friendly practices that protect marine biodiversity! Work alongside local communities to promote sustainable fishing techniques, reduce bycatch, and ensure our oceans remain a thriving source of life for generations to come.

Marine Wildlife Rescue & Rehabilitation

Be part of a life-saving mission! Assist experts in rescuing, treating, and rehabilitating injured sea turtles, dolphins, and seabirds affected by pollution and climate change. Your compassion and care can help return these beautiful creatures to the wild.

Water Quality Monitoring

Become a citizen scientist and contribute to ocean health research! Collect and analyze water samples, track pollution levels, and provide valuable data that can drive policy changes. Your efforts can help safeguard clean and thriving marine environments.
